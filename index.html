Hello! this is me

I’m passionate about three things: coding, photography, and Icelandic sheep. Whether I’m building software, capturing moments through my lens, or gathering sheep from the Icelandic mountains, I love blending creativity with technical skill. Programming allows me to solve complex problems, photography lets me explore the world with a unique perspective, and working with Icelandic sheep connects me to nature in a fun, healthy way. In everything I do, I aim for precision, clarity, and creativity.

Sports Enthusiast

Outside of work, sports are a big part of my life. Staying active helps me stay focused and balanced. As a former member of a national floorball team, I’ve experienced the thrill of competition and the power of teamwork at the highest level. Working with others to achieve a common goal drives my passion for collaboration and determination.

Farm Roots

I grew up on a farm in the south of Iceland. Our family farm, which includes two cottages for tourists, dairy cows, and sheep, taught me the importance of responsibility from an early age. Whether it was handling unexpected machine failures or managing unpredictable animal behavior, I learned how to stay calm under pressure and find practical solutions. These experiences shaped my work ethic and ability to adapt to challenging situations.

My family

I share my life with my wonderful girlfriend, Arndís, and our son, Valtýr, who was born in early August 2024. We enjoy taking walks, visiting my parents' farm, and having dinners with our families. Valtýr is already getting used to the sound of my camera clicking as I capture moments of our life together.
